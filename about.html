The Origin of Fatal Universe

"Fatal" is the second name of **AMINE**

A 19-years-old dedicated to the mechanical world.

Fatal Universe is the creative outlet of Amine. Obsessed with engineering, design, and raw power, Amine seeks to translate the technical perfection and aggression of the automotive world into photographic art. My focus is on the raw emotion, aggressive lines, and fleeting moments that define high-performance cars, giving every machine the gravity, mood, and cinematic feel it deserves.
